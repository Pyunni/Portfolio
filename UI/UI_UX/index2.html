Keeper

Keeper is the brain baby of my fellow classmate Brian Chiang, I was selected as part of the group as a middle man to help code, design, and manage the project.

The thought behind the idea was that a person could store their receipts on the keeper app and be able to pull it up when the need to make a return at a store.

This also helps the person to organize their receipts, set a budget, remind them when a receipt is expiring, and keep track of when they bought what.

The final product of the initial idea was slightly skewed but overall still matched the idea.
